noticed or remembered

1	If you place a macro parameter in the command position to execute or expand it

		p(2, 1)

	You can't have spaces in it like that, because masmx assemble isn't clever
	about parentheses at that point. You must do

		p(2,1)

	or you get a error which is hard to understand as it tries to swallow space
	as a subfunction

	might be worth the change


2	if you are dumb and do

	$if

	$else

		$elseif	. this should be $if

		$endif
	$endif

	It's not noticed by masmx and all subsequent conditions behave as if they were
	all true at once (you take every yes in a row of $elseif)

	It's not easy to see what you've done and maybe should be fixed

	It's already fixed if you don't have enough $endif
